Create your own font 😎

  • To use your handwriting, you will have to generate font of your handwriting.
  • Visit Calligraphr to make your own handwriting font.
  • See this video Here for detailed steps & leave a thumbs up for that guy as well 😉.
  • Once you get .ttf or .otf file of your handwriting, upload it from 'Upload your handwriting font' button.

Or download some default fonts

  • There are already created handwritten fonts, for you to save some time 😁.
  • Check out Handwriting Fonts to download free handwriting fonts.
  • Make sure to use Free License fonts only 😊.
  • Once you get .ttf or .otf file of your handwriting, upload it from 'Upload your handwriting font' button.
